# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 2026**

Batch Split PDFs is developed by [3thousand30](https://3thousand30.com).

---

## What we collect

3thousand30 does not collect, store, or receive your files or split output through our own servers.

- No analytics sent to us
- No telemetry sent to us
- No crash reporting sent to us
- No account or registration required

---

## What may leave your device

Your PDF files are processed entirely on your device. The app does not upload files to any server.

The app may request the **Inter** user-interface font from **Google Fonts** for display consistency. That request is made directly from your device to Google's servers and may include standard network information such as your IP address, subject to Google's own privacy practices. If Google Fonts is unavailable, the app falls back to local system fonts.

---

## Data stored on your device

- Your last used settings (output folder, pages per file, skip existing preference) — stored locally in `%APPDATA%\batchsplitpdf\`
- Output PDF files — written to the output folder you choose

This information remains on your device.
